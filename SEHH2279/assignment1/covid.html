Precaution Measure

Due to the outbreak of the Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19), Wind salon is taking extra precautions to protect the health of every salon guest to include health history review and enhanced sanitation/disinfection procedures according the policey of Hong Kong Governement.

Client Safety Guideline

Wearing mask

Visitors to the salon are required to wear face masks at all times. We cannot guarantee its safety during your cut or color service. If necessary, we can provide a disposable mask

Temperature

Before check in, a temperature screening will be conducted. Customers who have a fever will not be able to have services and will be requested to reschedule.

Service

We will not be offering beverages or food inside the salon. Also, we do not provide dry service and message service during the spread of covid-19

Stylist Safety Guideline

Staff Training

Our members have been certified in COVID-19 sanitation and disinfection procedures, and we promise to maintain social distancing between guests.

Ventilation

We have installed a Ultraviolet Light into our company to ensure fresh air and proper ventilation throughout the salon.

Sanitization

We will be frequently sanitizing frequently used surfaces, and our cut and color capes are used in once and are washed in hot water after each guest.
